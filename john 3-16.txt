                    A Love That Never Fails

                    For God so loved the world, He gave,  
                    His Son, that we might be saved.  
                    On Calvary’s cross, with arms outstretched wide,  
                    He took our sins, and in love, died.  

                    That whosoever, in faith, believes,  
                    In His great mercy, soul reprieves.  
                    Eternal life, a gift so pure,  
                    In Christ, our hope shall long endure.  

                    O wondrous love, beyond all measure,  
                    A Savior's grace, our deepest treasure.  
                    Through Him, we live, through Him, we stand,  
                    Forever held in God's own hand.
